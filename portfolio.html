About me

Welcome to my webpage. I am currently training to become a Full-stack web and software developer at Columbia University, School of Engineering, bootcamp.

I have a divese background. I am a medical doctor by profession, with residency training in surgery. I have also completed Masters in biostatistics from Johns Hopkins University.

Over the past 10 years, I have also worked as a physician scientist and biostatistician at Cleveland Clinic, Ohio, with specialization in the field of Heart and Vascular Surgery. I continued my work at Case Western Reserve University and Ohio State University with focus in cancer research.

During my tenure, I mastered the art of performing statistical analysis on large, complex databases including medicare data, National Cancer database etc, that brought to fruition many projects with publications in high impact journals
